Hello New Year!

While a bit belated, Happy New Year friends! I hope 2015 has already had a great start for you. It feels like the holiday season came and went in one big blur. I spent the week of Christmas in both Kentucky and Tennessee with my extended family and in-laws. My husband and I left the day after Christmas to go on a cruise to the Caribbean which spanned over New Year’s Eve–it was fantastic.


Now that I’m back to my regular schedule it’s time to start thinking the direction of my business this coming year. My main goals are to 1) continue to grow my early childhood classes, 2) establish a new contract in the geriatric field, 3) hire an assistant, 4) and finally to focus on creating worthwhile and creative content for you!


I’ve got some fun research articles and song ideas to share as well as some speaking engagements and presentations coming up that you can attend for free! I hope this year brings you nothing but the best. I’ll be in touch soon.

Free Day of Music This Saturday


The Nashville Symphony is hosting it’s annual free day of music THIS Saturday, September 27th. Hopefully you can attend as it is always a blast! Here are the highlights from their site:


The perfect way to celebrate a fall day! We invite the entire community to enjoy free musical performances all day and into the night at Schermerhorn Symphony Center. During Free Day of Music, we shine a light on some of the most talented and up-and-coming musical talent in Nashville, showcasing what makes Music City truly great.



  • A Grand Opening with the 101st Airborne Division Band at 10:15 a.m. on One Symphony Place

  • Doors open at 10:30, Music from 11 a.m. – 9 p.m.

  • Fun for the whole family

  • Over 20 acts on 4 stages performing classical, country, bluegrass, barbershop, rock, jazz, singer-songwriters, and so much more!
